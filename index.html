Darkhtrone is black metal band from Norway, their best albums consist of:

  • Panzerfaust
  • A Blaze in the northern sky
  • Transilvanian Hunger

Darkthrone was first a death metal band formed by Gylve Nagell and Anders Risberget in 1986 and going with the name Black Death. They released one demo entitled Trash Core '87. Ivar Enger later joined and they released a second demo, Black Is Beautiful which featured many guest musicians. The band then decided to go from death to black metal, recruited Dag Nilsen and changed their name to Darkthrone taking inspiration from a Celtic Frost song. The four then recorded another demo called Land of Frost.
